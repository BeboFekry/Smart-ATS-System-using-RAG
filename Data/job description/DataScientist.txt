## **Data Scientist – Job Description**

**Location:** Remote / On-site (Cairo, Egypt)
**Employment Type:** Full-time
**Department:** Data Science / Analytics
**Seniority Level:** Mid-Level

---

### **About the Role**

We are looking for a talented and analytical **Data Scientist** to join our dynamic data team. The ideal candidate will extract meaningful insights from complex datasets, design predictive models, and support data-driven decision-making across the organization. You will work closely with stakeholders to understand business problems and transform them into data solutions that deliver measurable value.

---

### **Key Responsibilities**

* Collect, clean, and preprocess large datasets from various sources.
* Apply statistical analysis and machine learning techniques to solve real-world problems.
* Build, validate, and deploy predictive models for classification, regression, clustering, or recommendation.
* Create dashboards and visualizations to communicate findings to non-technical stakeholders.
* Design and execute A/B tests to measure business impact.
* Translate business needs into technical requirements and data solutions.
* Document processes, models, and workflows for reproducibility and collaboration.

---

### **Required Qualifications**

* Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Data Science, Statistics, Computer Science, Mathematics, or a related field.
* Proficiency in **Python** (or R) with hands-on experience using libraries like **Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn, XGBoost, or StatsModels**.
* Strong knowledge of **statistics, hypothesis testing, and data exploration** techniques.
* Experience with **data visualization tools** (e.g., Matplotlib, Seaborn, Plotly, Tableau, or Power BI).
* Familiarity with **SQL** and working with relational databases.
* Ability to translate data insights into business actions and communicate clearly with cross-functional teams.

---

### **Preferred Skills**

* Experience with **time series analysis**, **NLP**, or **unsupervised learning** (clustering, dimensionality reduction).
* Exposure to **Big Data frameworks** like Spark or Hadoop is a plus.
* Experience with **cloud platforms** (AWS, GCP, or Azure).
* Familiarity with **MLOps tools**, version control (Git), and experiment tracking (MLflow, Weights & Biases).

---

### **Why Join Us?**

* Work on high-impact projects in a data-driven culture.
* Collaborate with top professionals in AI, engineering, and business.
* Competitive compensation, flexible working environment, and continuous learning support.
* Opportunity to work on diverse datasets and challenging data science problems.
