## **Human Resources (HR) Specialist – Job Description**

**Location:** Cairo, Egypt
**Employment Type:** Full-time
**Department:** Human Resources
**Seniority Level:** Entry to Mid-Level

---

### **About the Role**

We are seeking a proactive and people-oriented **HR Specialist** to support the full employee lifecycle—from recruitment and onboarding to performance management and employee relations. The ideal candidate will help foster a positive work environment, ensure HR compliance, and contribute to building a strong organizational culture.

---

### **Key Responsibilities**

* Coordinate and support recruitment processes, including job postings, candidate screening, and scheduling interviews.
* Manage employee onboarding and offboarding processes.
* Maintain accurate and up-to-date employee records and HR databases.
* Assist with payroll processing and benefits administration.
* Support performance appraisal cycles and training initiatives.
* Handle employee relations matters with professionalism and confidentiality.
* Ensure compliance with labor laws and internal policies.
* Organize HR events, workshops, and engagement activities.

---

### **Required Qualifications**

* Bachelor’s degree in Human Resources, Business Administration, or a related field.
* Proven experience in an HR role (internship or full-time).
* Knowledge of **labor laws**, HR policies, and best practices.
* Proficient in **Microsoft Office Suite** and HR software or systems (e.g., SAP, BambooHR, Zoho People).
* Strong interpersonal and communication skills.
* High level of confidentiality, empathy, and professionalism.

---

### **Preferred Skills**

* Familiarity with **recruitment platforms** like LinkedIn, Wuzzuf, or Forasna.
* Experience in **employee engagement** or **training & development**.
* Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and handle multiple priorities.
* Good command of **English** for internal communications and reporting.

---

### **Why Join Us?**

* Be part of a growing and supportive HR team.
* Play a vital role in shaping employee experience and company culture.
* Enjoy a dynamic workplace with career growth opportunities.
* Competitive salary, flexible environment, and professional training.
