## **Accountant – Job Description**

**Location:** Cairo, Egypt
**Employment Type:** Full-time
**Department:** Finance & Accounting
**Seniority Level:** Entry to Mid-Level

---

### **About the Role**

We are looking for a detail-oriented and reliable **Accountant** to join our finance team. The successful candidate will be responsible for managing daily accounting tasks, ensuring financial records are accurate, and supporting monthly and annual financial reporting. This role is essential to maintain the financial health and compliance of the organization.

---

### **Key Responsibilities**

* Prepare and maintain accurate accounting records, including general ledger entries and journal postings.
* Handle accounts payable and receivable, ensuring timely invoicing and payments.
* Assist in monthly closing procedures and reconciliation of bank statements.
* Prepare financial reports, balance sheets, and profit and loss statements.
* Ensure compliance with local tax regulations and assist in tax return preparation.
* Collaborate with auditors during internal and external audits.
* Support budgeting and forecasting processes.

---

### **Required Qualifications**

* Bachelor’s degree in Accounting, Finance, or a related field.
* Strong knowledge of accounting principles and financial reporting standards.
* Proficiency in **Microsoft Excel** and accounting software (e.g., **QuickBooks, SAP, or Odoo**).
* Attention to detail with excellent organizational and analytical skills.
* Ability to handle sensitive, confidential information with integrity.

---

### **Preferred Skills**

* Experience with **ERP systems** or digital accounting tools.
* Familiarity with **tax regulations and Egyptian accounting standards**.
* Basic understanding of payroll processing is a plus.
* Good command of **English** for reporting and communication.

---

### **Why Join Us?**

* Join a collaborative finance team with growth opportunities.
* Gain experience in financial management within a fast-paced industry.
* Competitive salary, professional development, and a supportive work environment.
