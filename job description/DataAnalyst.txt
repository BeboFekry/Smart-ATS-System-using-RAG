## **Data Analyst – Job Description**

**Location:** Hybrid (Cairo, Egypt)
**Employment Type:** Full-time
**Department:** Business Intelligence / Analytics
**Seniority Level:** Entry to Mid-Level

---

### **About the Role**

We are seeking a detail-oriented and business-savvy **Data Analyst** to join our analytics team. You will be responsible for gathering, processing, and analyzing data to provide actionable insights that support strategic decision-making. The ideal candidate is passionate about turning raw data into clear, concise stories through visualizations and reports.

---

### **Key Responsibilities**

* Collect and analyze data from multiple sources, ensuring accuracy and completeness.
* Develop dashboards and reports to visualize key performance indicators (KPIs) and business trends.
* Perform ad-hoc analyses to support cross-functional teams in marketing, operations, and finance.
* Identify patterns, trends, and correlations in large data sets to uncover business opportunities.
* Collaborate with stakeholders to define data requirements and reporting needs.
* Prepare and present findings to both technical and non-technical audiences.

---

### **Required Qualifications**

* Bachelor’s degree in Data Analytics, Business, Computer Science, Economics, or a related field.
* Strong proficiency in **SQL** and experience with **Excel** for data manipulation.
* Familiarity with **data visualization tools** such as **Tableau, Power BI, or Looker**.
* Experience in **data cleaning, transformation, and exploratory data analysis**.
* Good understanding of **descriptive statistics** and **business metrics**.
* Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.

---

### **Preferred Skills**

* Experience with **Python or R** for data analysis and automation.
* Knowledge of **ETL processes** and working with **databases** (PostgreSQL, MySQL).
* Familiarity with **A/B testing**, **segmentation**, or **customer analytics**.
* Understanding of cloud-based data environments (e.g., AWS Redshift, Google BigQuery).

---

### **Why Join Us?**

* Work in a data-driven organization where insights shape strategic direction.
* Gain exposure to real-world business problems and large-scale datasets.
* Opportunity for growth in analytics, data science, or business intelligence tracks.
* Flexible working hours, supportive team, and continuous professional development.
