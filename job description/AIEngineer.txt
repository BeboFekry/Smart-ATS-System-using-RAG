## **AI Engineer – Job Description**

**Location:** Cairo, Egypt
**Employment Type:** Full-time
**Department:** Artificial Intelligence / Data Science
**Seniority Level:** Mid to Senior Level

### **About the Role**

We are seeking a highly motivated and skilled **AI Engineer** to join our growing Artificial Intelligence team. The ideal candidate will design, develop, and implement AI-driven solutions that enhance business processes, drive innovation, and deliver high-impact outcomes. You will collaborate with cross-functional teams to turn data into actionable insights using cutting-edge machine learning and deep learning techniques.

---

### **Key Responsibilities**

* Design and implement machine learning and deep learning models for classification, regression, NLP, or computer vision tasks.
* Build and deploy scalable AI/ML pipelines in production environments.
* Collaborate with data engineers and software developers to integrate AI models into applications and products.
* Conduct data preprocessing, feature engineering, and model evaluation.
* Develop, maintain, and document AI tools and systems following software engineering best practices.
* Analyze model performance and iterate to improve accuracy and efficiency.
* Research and stay up to date with recent AI advancements and industry trends.

---

### **Required Qualifications**

* Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, or related field.
* Strong programming skills in **Python** and familiarity with libraries such as **TensorFlow, PyTorch, Scikit-learn, Transformers, or OpenCV**.
* Experience with **model training, tuning, and evaluation**.
* Solid understanding of **machine learning algorithms, deep learning architectures, and data preprocessing techniques**.
* Proficiency in working with **structured and unstructured data**, including images, text, and tabular data.
* Experience deploying models using **REST APIs, Flask/FastAPI, Docker, or cloud platforms** (AWS, GCP, Azure).
* Familiarity with **version control systems** (e.g., Git) and **ML lifecycle tools** like MLflow or Weights & Biases.

---

### **Preferred Skills**

* Experience with **NLP techniques** such as BERT, LLMs, or RAG-based architectures.
* Familiarity with **Vector Databases** (Chroma, FAISS, Pinecone).
* Experience with **MLOps**, model monitoring, and CI/CD pipelines for AI.
* Knowledge of **big data tools** like Spark, Kafka, or Databricks is a plus.

---

### **Why Join Us?**

* Work on real-world AI problems with immediate business impact.
* Opportunity to grow with a fast-paced, AI-first organization.
* Competitive salary, flexible work hours, and a collaborative team environment.
* Access to cloud credits, online courses, and AI research resources.
